Caesar Salad with Poached Egg.

This is a dish consisting of Scotch eggs and salad. Scotch eggs, a classic British dish, is made by wrapping a hard-boiled egg in a layer of savory sausage meat. The egg is then breaded and fried until golden. Alongside the Scotch eggs is a fresh salad consisting of lettuce leaves, sliced purple onions, and maybe some croutons.

Everything is served with a creamy mayonnaise or aioli dressing on the side. The dish combines a crunchy texture and rich savory flavor, often served as an appetizer or snack at events.

Mixed Berries Almond Nutella Pao.

This is a sweet version of baozi, which is a popular steamed bun in Asia. This chocolate pao is filled with rich melted chocolate,

giving a modern twist to a traditional dish. The buns are soft and chewy with a smooth surface, which contrasts with the smooth, melting chocolate filling. This dish is often favored by chocolate lovers and can be used as a snack or dessert on various occasions. The presentation is enhanced by the chocolate sauce garnish that is streaked underneath the pao, adding a decorative element and additional flavor.
